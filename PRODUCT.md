# PRODUCT.md

## Register

Brand.

## Product Purpose

This site is the public portfolio of Mamadou Oury Diallo. It must make his work legible to cultural institutions, XR clients, education partners, incubators and technical collaborators.

The portfolio should show one clear idea: Oury builds immersive systems where technology helps people learn, remember, train and transmit culture.

## Primary Audiences

- Cultural institutions and museum programs looking for immersive heritage work.
- Saboutech, Musée Virtuel de Guinée and similar grant evaluators.
- XR, Unity and 3D interactive clients.
- Education partners, bootcamps, universities and training programs.
- Creative technology studios and technical collaborators.

## Brand Promise

Immersive technology with cultural weight, technical discipline and field experience.

## Positioning

Mamadou Oury Diallo is not presented only as a developer. He is positioned as:

- XR and Unity builder.
- Creative technologist.
- Instructor and program lead.
- Heritage and education oriented maker.
- Bridge between African cultural material, interactive media and production-ready software.

## Core Proofs

Mandatory proof areas:

- Micro-Folie and digital artwork work with Institut français, Musée Théodore Monod, ASHIA and partners.
- Langa Bouri.
- Grande Vadrouille VR.
- Guinea 360 captures from Conakry, Lélouma, Labé and Korbé.
- Africa Digital Academy and Unity training in Conakry.
- VetiSimVR or similar VR simulator proof if assets are available.

Avoid for now:

- KOA.

## Content Strategy

English is primary. French is secondary.

Every project page should answer:

- What was built.
- Why it mattered.
- Oury's role.
- Tools and stack.
- Constraints.
- Result or proof.
- Media evidence.

If a project does not have enough proof yet, keep it as a short entry or hold it back.

## Site Shape

Expected pages:

- Home.
- Work.
- Case study pages.
- Teaching.
- Field Archive.
- About.
- Contact.

Expected interactive modules:

- Project index with strong filtering.
- Case study media rail.
- 360 viewer for selected Guinea captures.
- 3D viewer for selected lightweight assets.
- Bilingual content switch.
- Contact block with email and social links.

## Success Criteria

The site succeeds if:

- A grant evaluator understands Oury's relevance in under 90 seconds.
- A technical partner sees real implementation ability, not only slogans.
- A cultural partner sees respect for context, people and language.
- The portfolio feels memorable without becoming heavy or performative.
- Media loads progressively and never blocks navigation.

## Non Goals

- Not a generic résumé website.
- Not a full WebGL showpiece where usability disappears.
- Not a landing page for Janngo Agency alone.
- Not a public page for unfinished confidential projects.
- Not a Git repository containing heavy media files.

## Risks

- Too much spectacle could dilute credibility.
- Too much text could make the site feel academic.
- Heavy videos and 3D assets could slow the site.
- Cultural claims must be precise and backed by proof.
- Bilingual content must not feel duplicated or mechanical.

## Product Principles

- Proof before claims.
- Field material before stock aesthetics.
- Human voice before synthetic polish.
- Technical confidence without jargon overload.
- Heritage treated as living context, not decoration.
